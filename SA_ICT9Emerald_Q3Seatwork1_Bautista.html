THE GODFATHER


The Godfather:

When a crime family a.k.a The Corleone Family is seen having the most of their power and control over New York city and such, we will see waht happens as the power that was once there is shown to be challenged and how the Corleone family would soon be affected by this.


The Godfather Characters:

Vito Corleone

The Godfather

Vito Corleone a.k.a "Don Vito" is a mafia boss in New York City during the years of 1972, he was originally from Italy, in Corleone Sicily. His name was Vito Andollini, his family was murdered when he was young at the early 1900s, and so he had changed his name and fled to America for a new life of crime.

Michael Corleone

Son of Vito Corleone

One of the children of Vito Corleone, Michael was supposed to be one of the so-called good ones, but unluckily for him and his family, he was too much like his father. He had the wits and gall of his father and ended up becoming the new Mafia Boss of the Corleone Family in New York. His reign as the new Mafia Boss is shown more in the 2nd and last movie of The Godfather Trilogy.

Santino "Sonny" Corleone

Son of Vito Corleone

Santino "Sonny" Corleone had the looks and anger of his father, he was immature and only sought women, power, and money. But he was a fantastic son and brother in the Corleone family. All you needed to know was to never get on his bad side or else his temper will set you ablaze.

Thomas "Tom" Hagen

Consigliere and head lawyer of Vito Corleone

Thomas Hagen was a vital character in Vito Corleone's business, he was the one in charge of not getting the Corleone Family in most troubles and giving them advice because of how smart he was, though he wasn't biologically a son of Vito, he was the adodptive son of Vito and Carmela Corleone (Vito's wife)

Constanzia "Connie" Corleone

Daughter of Vito Corleone

Constanzia "Connie" Corleone was the only daughter of Vito Corleone, she had the power and selfless spirit to be able to lead the family, but due to her being a woman, she was not given that chance but instead was married to a man that abused her.

Frederico "Fredo" Corleone

Son of Vito Corleone

Frederico "Fredo" Corleone was the black sheep of the family, he was often seen as the coward and the one least likely to get the seat of becoming the boss of the Corleone family. He was seen as someone easily thwarted and something extreme even happens in a plot twist, for you to know what happened to Fredo, you must watch the Godfather movies.

Carmela Corleone

Wife of Vito Corleone

Carmela Corleone was seen as a sweet and respectable woman, Vito had met her after he had moved and grown accustomed to New York. They lived and gained their power together with their 5 children.
